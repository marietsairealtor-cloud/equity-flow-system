=== Renewal / Restore Behavior ===
Renew before archive: workspace resumes automatically after billing sync
Renew after archive: billing alone does not reopen workspace
Owner must click Restore workspace after billing is active again
Restore calls restore_workspace_v1(p_restore_token) -- backend authoritative
On success: entitlements refreshed, routing returns to Today
Renew after hard delete: no recovery
